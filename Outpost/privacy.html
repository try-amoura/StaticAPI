Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 22, 2026

Samyak Pawar ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Outpost mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use Outpost. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Because Outpost is designed strictly for reading news, we do not require users to create an account, and we do not collect direct personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number.

However, to ensure the App functions properly and to understand how users interact with our content, we automatically collect certain device and usage data:

  • Device Data: Information such as your device type, operating system, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.
  • Usage Data: Information on how you interact with the App, including pages viewed, time spent on articles, and crash reports.

2. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services to monitor app performance, analyze usage, and serve advertisements. These third parties may use cookies, web beacons, and similar tracking technologies to collect data about your activity.

  • Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics to collect usage data and improve the App's user experience. Read Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Ads (AdMob): The App may display advertisements provided by Google AdMob. Google uses advertising ID tracking to serve personalized ads based on your interests. You can opt-out of personalized advertising in your device settings.

3. General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) Compliance

If you are a resident of the European Economic Area (EEA), you have certain data protection rights regarding the device and analytics data collected:

  • The right to access, update, or delete the information we have on you.
  • The right of rectification (to correct inaccurate data).
  • The right to object to our processing of your data.
  • The right of restriction (to request we restrict data processing).
  • The right to data portability.
  • The right to withdraw consent at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, or to opt-out of analytics and advertising tracking, please adjust your device's tracking permissions (via iOS Settings or Android Settings) or contact us directly.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain usage and analytics data only for as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy. Because we do not maintain user accounts, there is no direct profile to delete. However, you can reset your device's advertising identifier or uninstall the App at any time to immediately cease all data collection.

5. Children's Privacy

Outpost does not knowingly collect any personal data from children under the age of 13 (or 16 in certain European jurisdictions). If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected such data, we will take reasonable steps to delete it immediately.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
