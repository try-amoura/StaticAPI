Privacy Policy

TicTacFlo

Last updated: August 27, 2025

Summary

TicTacFlo does not create user accounts or collect personal information on our servers. The app shows banner ads only using Google AdMob, which may collect device-level data to deliver and measure ads.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit personal data on our servers. The game runs entirely on your device. Single‑player scores are saved locally using Apple’s UserDefaults.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

We use Google AdMob to display banner advertisements. Google and its advertising partners may collect information such as:

  • Device identifiers (e.g., Apple’s Advertising Identifier “IDFA”)
  • Approximate location, IP address, and device information (model, OS version)
  • App usage and ad interaction data (impressions, clicks), and diagnostic information

This data helps deliver, limit, and measure ads, and is also used for fraud prevention. Processing of this data is governed by Google’s policies.
